Our Goals

Mission

To enable students to explore more into the world of computer programming and STEM through practical hands-on experience and collaborative learning and research. We hope to broaden the reach of students in today's modern society that is growing more reliant on technology and advance those who are willing to in the field of computer science and information technologies.

Vision

To create a learning environment that enriches students in the field of Computer Science and Technology to inspire innovation and creativity within our school community, and to enable students to explore their interests and curiosities.
